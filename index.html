🏫 About Our School

A Legacy of Excellence in Education

First Cry Convent School was established in 2005 with a vision to provide world-class education to students of Chhatarpur, Madhya Pradesh. Over two decades, we have grown into one of the most respected educational institutions in the region.

We follow the CBSE curriculum and focus on holistic development — academic excellence, extracurricular activities, sports, and character building — to prepare students for a competitive world.

🎓

CBSE Affiliated Institution

Nationally recognized curriculum with experienced educators.
